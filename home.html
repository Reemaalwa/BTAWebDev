We are BTA

Telfer Business and Technology Association

A community of passionate students who share a love for innovation and the world of business. Whether you're into coding, marketing, finance, or anything in between, we've got something for you.

What we do

We host industry talks, networking events, and workshops designed to expand your knowledge and connect you with like-minded peers.

Let's shape the future of business together!

Our Mission

To empower students to reach their full potential by providing opportunities for personal and professional growth.

Our Values

Integrity, Innovation, Inclusivity, and Impact.

Our Vision

To be the leading student organization that connects students with industry professionals and fosters a culture of innovation and collaboration.

Our History

Founded in 2015, the Telfer Business and Technology Association (BTA) is a student-run organization that aims to connect students with industry professionals and provide opportunities for personal and professional growth.
